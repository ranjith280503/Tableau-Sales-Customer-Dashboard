# 📊 Sales & Customer Dashboard | Tableau

## Overview

This project consists of two interactive Tableau dashboards designed to analyze business performance and customer behavior. The dashboards provide meaningful insights through KPI cards, trend analysis, and interactive visualizations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 📈 Sales Dashboard

The **Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance and business growth.

### Features

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Sales per Customer
* Weekly Sales Trends
* Sub-Category Performance
* Interactive Filters
* Dynamic KPI Cards

---

# 👥 Customer Dashboard

The **Customer Dashboard** focuses on customer analytics and purchasing behavior.

### Features

* Total Customers
* Sales per Customer
* Top Customers
* Customer Distribution
* Customer Purchase Analysis
* Interactive Filters
* Customer Insights
