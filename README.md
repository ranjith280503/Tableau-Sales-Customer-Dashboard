# 📊 Sales & Customer Dashboard | Tableau

## Overview

This project is an interactive **Sales & Customer Dashboard** developed in **Tableau** to analyze business performance through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking. The dashboard enables users to monitor sales trends, customer performance, profitability, and order metrics with interactive filters and drill-down capabilities.

---

# Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Track customer growth and purchasing behavior.
* Monitor business KPIs.
* Compare product and sub-category performance.
* Identify top customers.
* Support business decisions with interactive dashboards.

---

# Dashboards

## 📈 Sales Dashboard

The Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance, including:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Sales per Customer
* Weekly Sales Trends
* Sub-Category Comparison
* Interactive Filters
* Dynamic KPI Cards
* Current year vs previous Year Sales
  
  

---

## 👥 Customer Dashboard

The Customer Dashboard focuses on customer analytics, including:

* Customer Distribution
* Top Customers
* Customer KPIs
* Customer Purchase Analysis
* Sales per Customer
* Interactive Customer Insights
*  Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Sales per Customer
